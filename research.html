Interaction of Lanthanide Ions with Amino Acids

Calculation of Interaction Energy and Free Energies between Lanthanide Ions and Amino Acids in a system of all lanthanide ions added in the system of single Amino Acid.

Tools Using: GROMACS, VMD, SuperComputer: ParamAnanta, CHARMM-GUI, XMGRACE, PLUMED

Peptide-Surface Compendium for the Interactions of Solid Binding Peptides with Solid Surfaces

The interactions of solid binding peptides with some specific solid surfaces are of great importance. Some are already found and it may happen that a gold binding peptide binds very well with some other solid surfaces. So, all the interactions are necessary to be seen. All the important analysis done on these interactions such as Interaction Energy, RMSD Average and RMSD Plots, RMSF Plots and Minimum Distance of each residue of the peptide with the surface will be shown on the website. Also, the final view of the system after the production run will be shown.

Tools Using: GROMACS, VMD, SuperComputer: ParamAnanta, CHARMM-GUI, XMGRACE, PLUMED
